Part 9 · Reference

Escalation, Thresholds & Contacts

Read this before you drive out. When one of these conditions is true, you call from the location — you don't wait for Wednesday.

Escalate immediately — do not wait for Wednesday

  • Gas detection is out of service or cannot be returned to service.
  • The alarm will not sound, or the alarm is not received remotely.
  • Communications are down and cannot be restored on site.
  • Any condition where the rig is running without working H₂S detection.
  • Any safety hazard, damaged equipment from an incident, or anything the company man raises.
  • Any part needed on an URGENT basis that is not in shop stock.

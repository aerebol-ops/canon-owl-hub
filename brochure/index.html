Trained Internally · Reinforced Weekly

The 12 Life-Saving Rules.

The PSMS topics every Canon crew is trained and tested on — digitized as controls they can pull up on-site, any time.

01

Energy Isolation

Verify isolation & zero stored energy before work.

02

Breaking Containment

Manage exposure to energy & hazardous substances.

03

Line of Fire

Keep yourself and others out of the line of fire.

04

Mobile Equipment

Separate people from moving equipment; use spotters.

05

Confined Space

Isolate, test the atmosphere, attendant & rescue ready.

06

Driving

Seatbelt on, phone down, fit and within the limit.

07

Hot Work

Control flammables and ignition sources; fire watch set.

08

Bypassing Controls

Use safety-critical equipment and procedures as designed.

09

Excavation

Authorized; utilities located, de-energized or protected.

10

Safe Lifting

Lift plan in place; never walk under a suspended load.

11

Working at Height

Protect against a fall; inspect and anchor before use.

12

Work Authorization

Permit confirmed, hazards controlled, safe to start.

Twelve rules. Zero shortcuts. Everyone goes home.
